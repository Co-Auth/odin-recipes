The Perfect PB&J



This simple yet effective PB&J recipe will leave you craving another after your last bite.


Ingredients


  • Two slices of bread of your choosing.
  • Peanut Butter (Your favorite brand)
  • Jelly (or jam at your discretion)

Steps to Perfection


  1. Remove lid from both the jar of Peanut Butter and the jar of jelly.
  2. Place your two slices of bread down on a napkin or plate.
  3. With a knife or other spreading utensil, lather some peanut butter onto one slice of bread.
  4. After wiping your knife with a napkin or cloth, lather some jelly/jam on the other slice of bread.
  5. Now, place both pieces of bread together, with the jelly side facing the peanut butter side!
  6. You may now enjoy your delicious PB&J!
